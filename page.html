About Us

This project developed by Valor focuses on creating Smart Parking Management System which addresses urban parking challenges like limited availability, wasted space, and time-consuming searches. Using real-time data from sensors, it monitors parking occupancy, guiding drivers to available spots through in-car infotainment systems or a mobile app. The app also allows users to reserve spaces and make cashless payments, simplifying the process. Future plans include automated valet parking for autonomous vehicle parking. Targeted at tech-savvy professionals, the system aims to optimize space, reduce congestion, and improve the urban parking experience.
